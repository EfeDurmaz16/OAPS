# Neutral By Design

## Purpose

This page records the suite's public neutrality posture.

## Meaning

Neutral by design means OAPS should:

- map to adjacent ecosystems without absorbing them
- reuse existing protocols where they already solve the narrow problem well
- avoid turning aligned systems into mandatory dependencies
- avoid framing itself as a replacement for ecosystems that already work
- keep its own primitives portable across implementations

## Why This Matters

The suite needs enough specificity to be useful, but not so much ecosystem capture that it becomes a disguised product spec.

Neutrality helps keep the standard adoptable by multiple adjacent communities.

## Current Aligned Systems

The repo already treats several systems as aligned rather than captive targets:

- Sardis for payment governance
- FIDES for trust and attestation
- agit for lineage and replay
- OSP for provisioning

## Copy Rule

Any public copy about OAPS should make the following distinction clear:

- OAPS standardizes shared control-plane semantics
- adjacent protocols and products continue to own their own transport and product UX
